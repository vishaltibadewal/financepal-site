Privacy Policy

Overview

FinancePal is designed with privacy as a core principle. All financial data you enter is stored exclusively on your device and, if you choose to enable it, in your personal iCloud account. We do not operate any servers, do not collect any data, and have no ability to access your information.

Data We Collect

We collect no data. FinancePal does not transmit any information to us or any third party.

Data Storage

All information you enter — including income, expenses, investments, and portfolio details — is stored locally on your iPhone in a secure, backup-excluded directory. If you enable iCloud sync in Settings, your data is synced via Apple's iCloud Key-Value Storage to your personal iCloud account. This data is accessible only to you.

Analytics and Tracking

FinancePal contains no analytics, no tracking, no advertising SDKs, and no third-party libraries of any kind.

Data Deletion

You can delete all data at any time using the "Clear All Data" option in the Settings tab. Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data.

Children's Privacy

FinancePal is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.
